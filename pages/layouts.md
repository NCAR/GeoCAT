## Layouts
### Default

The only front matter information required for default pages is:

```yml
---
layout:
title:
---
```

### Front page

Front matter options for the front page are:

```yml
---
layout:
title:
banner-title:
banner-description:
banner-button-text:
banner-button-url:
---
```
